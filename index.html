FAQ

Common Questions

What areas does Bertoni Clean serve?
We serve all of Orange County, including Irvine, Newport Beach, Huntington Beach, Costa Mesa, Laguna Beach, Mission Viejo, Lake Forest, Aliso Viejo, Anaheim, Santa Ana, Fullerton, Tustin, Dana Point, and more. Not sure if we cover your city? Reach out — we likely do.
How do I book a cleaning?
Simply fill out the contact form above, email us at bertoniclean@gmail.com, or call us directly. We'll confirm your appointment and customize the scope of your clean within 24 hours.
Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not at all. Many of our clients provide us with access and carry on with their day. We handle everything and lock up securely when we're done.
What is the difference between a standard clean and a deep clean?
A standard clean covers regular maintenance — dusting, vacuuming, mopping, and surface cleaning. A deep clean goes further: behind appliances, inside cabinets, grout scrubbing, baseboards, and every overlooked detail. We recommend a deep clean for first-time clients or seasonal resets.
Do you bring your own supplies?
Yes. We arrive fully equipped with professional-grade cleaning products and equipment. If you have a preference for specific products (eco-friendly, fragrance-free, etc.), let us know and we'll accommodate.
